About

Fed up of all the boring health apps which are no where helping you to maintain a healthy diet plan? Switch to funfit- now maintaining good health's a fun. Funfit drives you to achieve your health goals by maintaining a healthy diet plan With the application of game elements Funfit makes achieving health objectives fun, highly addictive and engaging. just upload the pictures of whatever you eat and unclock the perks in the game which include level up, higher jumps, and more daily lives based on the nutritional values of your food. As our application also shows the daily leaderboard based upon your scores, it motivates you stick to healthy food only as much as possible and attain a healthy lifestyle.
